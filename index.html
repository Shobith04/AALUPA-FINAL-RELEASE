Our Heritage

The name "Aalupa" is derived from the Alupa dynasty—an ancient royal lineage that ruled the Tulu Nadu region for over a thousand years. Known for its strong governance, cultural richness and maritime foresight, the dynasty inspires our commitment to rooted values, structured systems and forward-looking vision.
